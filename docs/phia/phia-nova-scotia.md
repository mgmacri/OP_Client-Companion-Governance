Purpose of Act
2

The purpose of  this Act  is to govern the  collection, use,  disclosure,
retention, disposal and destruction of personal health information in a manner that
recognizes both the right of individuals to protect their personal health information
and the need of custodians to collect, use and disclose personal health information
to provide, support and manage health care.  2010, c. 41, s. 2.
